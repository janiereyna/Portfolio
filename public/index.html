About Arianna

Pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from University of North Texas, I have gained a solid foundation in programming principles and software development methodologies.

I'm a software engineering student with a passion for creating innovative solutions through technology. As a student, I have honed my skills through various courses, projects, and internships. I have worked on a website called Color-Vision, a react app called UNT-Rideshare App, which have allowed me to gain practical experience in web and mobile app development. Additionally, I am currently working towards earning my Cybersecurity certificate and a minor in mathematics. I am excited about the endless possibilities technology offers and the ability to make a positive impact on society. I am eager to continue learning, growing, and contributing to the technology industry through my work.
